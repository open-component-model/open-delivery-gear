# Motivation

Compliance is not living next to Software, instead is an integral part of it.
The way we are integrating Compliance into Software is by building automations naturally integrated into the Software Lifecycle.

## Motivation



- security and compliance requirements have to be fulfilled
- security and compliance requirements continously change
- automation is required to scale the processes
- processes must be decoupled from tooling (scanners)
- assessment data must be transportable
